# **Apply to Join**

*Forum membership in the Ard Society — the first step*

If you have read the documents on this site and found in them something you believe to be true — the philosophy, the project, the governance model — then this is where you take the first step. Forum membership is the entry point into the Ard Society community. It requires nothing more than an honest introduction and a willingness to engage seriously.

We do not want a polished application. We want an honest one. The questions below are designed to give us a sense of who you are and why you are here. Answer them in your own voice, without performing. The people we are looking for tend to write as they think — directly and without pretension.

* **The Three Questions** *

Your application should answer the following three questions. There is no minimum or maximum length — write as much or as little as the question requires. A few thoughtful paragraphs on each is the right register. An essay is not necessary; a sentence is not sufficient.

> **1.  Who are you?**

- *Not a curriculum vitae — a person. Tell us something true about yourself: your background, your work, your interests, the things that have shaped how you think. You may use your real name or a handle; we will ask for more identifying information only if and when the relationship warrants it.*

> **2.  What brought you here?**

- *How did you find the Ard Society, and what in the documents resonated with you — or challenged you? We are interested in your honest reaction to what you read, including disagreements. The quality of your engagement with the ideas matters more than agreement with them.*

> **3.  What do you bring?**

- *This is not a request for your credentials — it is a question about your contribution. What skills, knowledge, perspective, or capacity do you bring to a project of this kind? What could you do, concretely, in a founding community that needs to build a society from the ground up?*

* **How to Apply** *

Send your application by email to the address below. Use the subject line: Application — Forum Membership. Write your answers to the three questions in the body of the email. No attachments are necessary at this stage.

**apply@ardtiresociety.org**

Applications are reviewed personally. You will receive a response — acceptance, decline, or a request for further conversation — within a reasonable period. We read every application in full. We do not use automated filters.

> There is no fee to apply and no fee for Forum membership. If you are ever asked to pay money to access the community before reaching Founding Member status through the processes described on this site, do not pay — and contact us immediately.

* **A Note on Response Time** *

The project is in its early stages and is run by a small team. We aim to respond to every application within two weeks. During periods of high volume this may extend somewhat. If you have not heard back within a month, a brief follow-up is appropriate.

A decline is not a permanent judgment. The community will develop over time, and what is not the right fit at one moment may be at another. If you receive a decline and believe it was in error — or if your circumstances change in relevant ways — you are welcome to apply again after a period of six months.
